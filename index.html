Apartment description

Apartment Žužić offers fully equipped air-conditioned apartment with free WiFi and private free parking in front of the apartment. Located near Novigrad, the property is 15 minutes from Umag and Poreč. The apartment has a living room with a TV and a equipped kitchen. Bed linen and towels are provided. Pula Airport is 75 km from the property and Portorož Airport, Sečovlje is 24 km away. Aquapark Istralandia is located 10 minutes from the building. See more about the apartment
