Opis apartmana

Apartment Žužić offers fully equipped air-conditioned apartment with free WiFi and private free parking in front of the apartment. Pula Airport is 75 km from the property and Portorož Airport, Sečovlje is 24 km away. Aquapark Istralandia is located 10 minutes from the building. Vidi više o apartmanu
